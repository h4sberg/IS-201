Tips for Effective Bench Pressing

  1. Ensure proper grip on the barbell
    • Place hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
    • Keep your wrists straight and strong.
  2. Use your legs for stability
    • Plant your feet firmly on the floor to generate power.
    • Engage your core to maintain stability during the lift.
  3. Keep your elbows at a 45-degree angle
  4. Focus on controlled movement
